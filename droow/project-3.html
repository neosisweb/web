Maintenance

Keeping Your Kuwait Garden Thriving

A beautiful garden requires consistent, knowledgeable care, especially in Kuwait's unique climate. Plants KW offers comprehensive garden maintenance services to ensure your outdoor space remains healthy, vibrant, and enjoyable throughout the year. Our expert team handles everything from lawn care and pruning to fertilization and pest control, tailored to the specific needs of your garden.

Our Approach

Our garden care services include regular mowing, edging, and lawn treatments to keep your grass lush and green. We provide expert pruning for shrubs, trees, and hedges to promote healthy growth and maintain their shape. Our team also offers tailored fertilization programs using high-quality nutrients to ensure your plants receive the nourishment they need. Additionally, we implement effective, environmentally-conscious pest and disease control measures to protect your garden. We also specialize in irrigation system checks and adjustments to ensure efficient water use, crucial for Kuwait's climate.
