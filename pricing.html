Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical timeline for a project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A Startup Spark package might take 2-4 weeks, while Business Boost can range from 4-8 weeks. Custom Enterprise projects have tailored timelines discussed during consultation.

Do you offer services to clients outside Kuwait?

Absolutely! While we are based in Kuwait, we proudly serve clients globally. We leverage digital communication tools to ensure seamless collaboration regardless of your location.

What kind of support do you offer after website launch?

Each package includes a defined support period. We also offer ongoing maintenance plans covering security updates, backups, performance checks, and technical assistance to ensure your website remains in top condition.

Can you help with website hosting?

Yes, we provide reliable web hosting solutions, including expertise with providers like Hostinger. We can help you choose the right plan and manage your hosting environment for optimal performance and security.
